ABOUT THE WEBSITE

The bustling capital of Ethiopia, Addis Ababa is very much the centre of this diverse and delightful country. For a start, it geographically sits in the middle of this large nation and, at 2355m above sea level, occupies an elevated position overseeing large swaths of the rest of the country. The city itself has a population of about 2.7 million people and the urban area of Addis Ababa is home to around 4.8 million people. Addis Ababa is also the cultural hub of Ethiopia, with several important museums stationed here, as well as major universities and many Ethiopian Orthodox sites and treasures. And Addis Ababa is also the business hub of this otherwise very rural country. It is at the north edge of Addis Ababa at an altitude of 2600m to 3200 meters. Now, the Park is fully delivering services with many indoor and outdoor adventure and recreational activities.
