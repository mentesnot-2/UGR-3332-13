The bustling capital of Ethiopia, Addis Ababa is very much the centre of this diverse and delightful country. For
a start, it geographically sits in the middle of this large nation and, at 2355m above sea level, occupies an elevated
position overseeing large swaths of the rest of the country. The city itself has a population of about 2.7 million
people and the urban area of Addis Ababa is home to around 4.8 million people. Addis Ababa is also the cultural hub of
Ethiopia, with several important museums stationed here, as well as major universities and many Ethiopian Orthodox
sites and treasures. And Addis Ababa is also the business hub of this otherwise very rural country. As such, it’s home to the
movers and shakers of this quick developing nation, the young and educated as well as a small but growing middle class.
